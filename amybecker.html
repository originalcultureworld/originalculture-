YOUR DJ AMY BECKER

London, 2019By Lamia

Meet met with Amy Becker, a trailblazing female DJ of our generation from London, who has been making waves since her early days in the underground scene. Starting from practising in her room to gaining entry to London clubs at just 15 years old, her story is nothing short of inspiring.
